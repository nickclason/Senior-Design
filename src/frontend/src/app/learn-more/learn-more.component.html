Add Widget

Use this widget to log all transactions made to your investing portfolio. By entering the information in this form, the portfolio will have all the necessary information to properly track your finances

Holdings Widget

Use this widget to view which financial assets you currently own. The stocks listed here are also reflected in the graph in the Portfolio Widget

Portfolio Widget

Use this widget to track the performance of your financial assets over time. For the data pertaining to a specific day, hover your cursor over a datapoint and note the price information on the tooltip.

Watchlist Widget

Use this widget to track your potential future investments. Add stocks to the list to view their price data, along with the ability to view each individual stock's market news and investment data

Treemap Widget

Use this widget to visualize the breakdown of markets in which you've invested. This is helpful in understanding your current investment diversity.

Prediction Widget

Use this widget to view generated predictions for an individual stock's upcoming price. This prediction is generated through creating an LSTM model for the analysis of historical price data.

House Widget

Use this widget track the investments that are made by public officials in the House of Representatives. House Reps are required to disclose their investments with the public; this information has been compiled into data that can be viewed in this tool. This information can help illustrate the direction a particular stock is heading, depending on policy makers' investment tendencies.

Senate Widget

Use this widget track the investments that are made by public officials in the Senate. Senators are required to disclose their investments with the public; this information has been compiled into data that can be viewed in this tool. This information can help illustrate the direction a particular stock is heading, depending on policy makers' investment tendencies.
